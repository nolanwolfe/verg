Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

Verg ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Only: Verg stores all your data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Session history (dates and durations of your writing sessions)
  • Photos of your journal pages (stored in your device's photo library)
  • App settings and preferences

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. Your journal photos and session data never leave your device.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics data to improve the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally and includes:

  • App usage patterns (e.g., which features are used most)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Device type and operating system version

Subscriptions

Verg offers subscription-based premium features. Subscriptions are processed and managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. For information about how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous subscription-related data. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security features, including device passcode and biometric authentication if enabled.

Children's Privacy

Verg is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
