Verg

A journaling app that gets you off your phone and onto paper.

Verg helps you build a daily writing habit. Instead of typing on a screen, you write with pen and paper while a candle burns down. When you're done, snap a photo to track your progress.

How It Works

1. Start the timer

Open Verg and tap to light the candle. Set your phone down.

2. Write on paper

Grab a pen and your journal. Write freely while the candle burns.

3. Capture your page

When you're finished, use the camera to photograph your journal page. Verg saves it so you can look back on your progress.

4. Build the habit

Track your streak and watch your journal grow over time.
